Quick Start Guide

Get your business up and running with KiraBiz Accounting Plus in minutes.

1 Installation

  1. Download the installer KiraBiz_Accounting_Plus_Setup.exe from your purchase link.
  2. Run the installer. If Windows SmartScreen appears, click "More Info" and then "Run Anyway".
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions. The application will install to your local user folder for maximum compatibility.
  4. Once finished, a shortcut will be created on your Desktop.

2 First Launch

Double-click the KiraBiz Accounting Plus icon on your desktop. The application will perform a brief background check and launch your default web browser in "App Mode".

üí° Tip: KiraBiz uses a background local server. Do not close the small command window if it appears, as this is the engine powering your software.

3 Company Setup

On the "Welcome" screen, you have two main options:

  • Open Sample Company: Explore the software with pre-loaded data (ABC Enterprise Sdn Bhd).
  • Create New Company: Start fresh with your own business. Enter your company name and choose an industry template to automatically generate your Chart of Accounts.

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Company Info to upload your logo and set your address for invoices.

4 License Activation

KiraBiz starts in Trial Mode. To unlock full features and remove watermarks:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Licensing.
  2. Enter your 5-digit Postcode.
  3. Click "Request via WhatsApp". This will automatically send your Unique Machine ID to our support team.
  4. Once you receive your Activation Key, paste it into the "Activation Key" box and click Activate Product.
